---
id: evidence-anchors-to-the-report
title: Anchor hypothesis evidence to at least one measured number
tags: [evidence, hypothesis, science-monitor, numbers, provenance]
audience: [strategizer]
---
When you update a hypothesis, cite evidence as a delegation id plus a small
dict of numbers: `evidence={"delegation": "D003", "numbers": {...}}`. At least
one of those numbers must appear in that delegation's report — that anchors the
claim to real data.

You may freely include DERIVED quantities you computed yourself alongside the
raw measurement — counts, classifications, ratios, reformatted coordinates.
Those will not appear verbatim in the worker's report, and that is fine: the
monitor only flags an update when NONE of its numbers are grounded in the
report (a sign the evidence is fabricated or cites the wrong delegation). Keep
your interpretation; just make sure a measured value is in there too.
